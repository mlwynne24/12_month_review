The Government Automation Forum

Goal: To create a more pin-point, client-centric method of generating opportunities.

Solution: The Government Automation Forum, a quarterly consortium where Technology Leaders in Government can discuss their automation experiences.

Results:

  • First event in April
  • 80% attendance from six different government agencies
  • Generated follow-up meetings with three agencies
  • Strong debate and participants agreed to reconvene in early Summer and beyond
