Wimbledon

I worked at Wimbledon for 3 weeks. In this time, I performed a multitude of tasks and took on an increasing amount of responsibility. These included:

  • Managing and coordinating the TV Graphics Team.
  • Ensuring all data-entry bags are equipped with all necessary equipment and the beginning and end of each day.
  • Greeting and liaising with corporate guests.
  • Various physical and administrative tasks.

I thoroughly enjoyed my experience, made some great friends and have been informally invited back to join the team again next year!
