Account-Based Marketing

After reaching out to the UK Marketing team, I was able to aid the new UKI Account-Based Marketing (ABM) team in gaining traction with their accounts. The activities I helped them with included:

  • Gaining a view of the install base and future opportunities of their accounts
  • Creating a full account-specific plan for each account, e.g. HSBC, Lloyds and BP
  • Research MDI to find account-insights, e.g. market size, competitors, potential challenges, etc.
  • Create marketing story diagrams to demonstrate the buying process for large accounts
